Music Compositions for Double Bass, Piano, and String Orchestra by Murray "Doc" Solomon

Murray "Doc" Solomon was born in The Big Apple, New York City, USA. He is a successful veteran double bassist, music educator and composer for the double bass, piano and string orchestra. In his childhood he played piano in Fox Movietone films and began to play the double bass upon entering Music & Art High School, studying with Fred Zimmerman of the New York Philharmonic Orchestra, one of the most important American pedagogues and transcribers of music for the double bass.
